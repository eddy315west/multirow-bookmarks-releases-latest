# Privacy Policy for Multirow Bookmarks

**Extension Name:** Multirow Bookmarks (5 Rows)  
**Developer:** Eddy315West  
**Contact:** GitHub Issues at https://github.com/eddy315west/multirow-bookmarks/issues

## Data Collection
This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing occurs locally in your browser.

## Permissions

### Bookmarks Permission
- **Purpose:** Read-only access to display your bookmarks toolbar items
- **Usage:** The extension reads bookmarks to show them in a multi-row grid popup
- **Data Handling:** Bookmarks are only displayed locally. No data leaves your browser.

## What We Don't Do
- We don't collect analytics
- We don't use cookies
- We don't track browsing history
- We don't sell or share data (we don't collect any)
- We don't have servers receiving user data
